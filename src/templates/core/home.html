Cities are where the struggle for global sustainability "will either be won or lost"

Cities consume 70% of global natural resources|generate 64% of global waste|do something else|are where many people live

Urban metabolism systematically assesses the sustainability of cities.

It measures material, waste, water, and energy flows and stock in cities

To be more successfully used, we need to collect more data|do research|eat candy|get down to business

Our mission it so collaborate on systematically improving the sustainability of cities.

We do this by creating and sharing urban metabolism knowledge and accelerating its implementation in policy and practive.

Create knowledge

We aim to create new knowledge by participating and collaborating in research projects and developing new open source tools.

Share knowledge

We aim to gather and curate (all) knowledge around urban metabolism in one central platform in order to make it accessible to different target audiences

Apply knowledge

We help policy makers and practitioners to tackle their challenges with Urban Metabolism thinking.

Foster a community

We believe that tackling the urban sustainability challenge can not be done alone. We want to foster a community of stakeholders that collaborates on developping solutions.
