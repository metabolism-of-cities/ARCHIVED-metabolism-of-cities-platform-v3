Material catalog

The principal catalog for reporting material flows is based on the Eurostat Material flow accounts (env_ac_mfa). The data structure used is version 14 March 2018. You can download the 2018 handbook here. If your materials can not be easily classified using this catalog, please look into the following alternative catalogs:

  • Harmonized system - useful if you have data on specific products
  • Engineering materials - different types of materials used in engineering; especially useful if you describe (part of) a production process
  • Elements - used when you are reporting on a specific element (like cobalt or silver)
